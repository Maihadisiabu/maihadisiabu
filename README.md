## About Me

I am a COREN-registered Computer Engineer and independent researcher specialising in
**Agricultural Robotics**, **Embedded AI**, **IoT Systems**, and **Cybersecurity**.

My work focuses on building intelligent, resource-constrained autonomous systems that
operate reliably in real-world field conditions -- without cloud compute, without
heavy infrastructure, and at costs accessible to smallholder farmers.

I am currently applying for a **Direct PhD** in Control and Instrumentation Engineering
at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM), with a proposed research
direction in adaptive embedded control for agricultural robots under Dr. Hassan Almubarak.
